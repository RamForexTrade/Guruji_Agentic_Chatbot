# Sri Sri Ravishankar Teaching #255

## Teaching #255: Life Without Wisdom is Incomplete
**Date:** June 1, 2000  
**Location:** Bangalore Ashram, India  
**Topics:** wisdom, feeling, action, fulfillment, self  
**Keywords:** wisdom, feeling, action, fulfillment, self, completeness, life  
**Problem Categories:** seeking_wisdom, understanding_life, spiritual_growth  
**Emotional States:** fulfillment, peace, wisdom  
**Life Situations:** general_guidance, spiritual_inquiry, self_development  

### Content:
Wisdom that doesn't give rise to feeling is incomplete.

Feeling that doesn't translate into action is incomplete.

Action that doesn't give rise to fulfillment is incomplete.

For Fulfillment, is returning to the Self.
