# Sri Sri Ravishankar Teaching #204

## Teaching #204: Shortcut!
**Date:** May 20, 1999  
**Location:** Bad Antogast, Germany  
**Topics:** enlightenment, stupidity, intelligence, self-knowledge  
**Keywords:** stupid, intelligent, enlightenment, self-knowledge, awareness, who am i  
**Problem Categories:** self_doubt, ego, seeking_enlightenment  
**Emotional States:** confusion, humor, self-awareness  
**Life Situations:** spiritual_seeking, self_inquiry  

### Content:
If you think you are stupid
Then...... you know who you are.
And if you know who you are......
You are enlightened!
And if you are enlightened,
Then ......you are certainly not stupid.

If you think you are intelligent,
Then you don't know who you are.
And if don't know who you are.....
Then you really ARE stupid!

Better realize your stupidity ....and Be Enlightened!!!!!!
